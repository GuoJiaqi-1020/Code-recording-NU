Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Enter the number of stars(n>1) you want to appear on the canvas. The higher the number, the more stars will appear on the screen at the same time

Drag the mouse to rotate the moon at the same time when the stars will follow the rotation.

(One thing to note is that the stars will rotate at different angles and may not be particularly easy to observe because they are flashing all the time)
