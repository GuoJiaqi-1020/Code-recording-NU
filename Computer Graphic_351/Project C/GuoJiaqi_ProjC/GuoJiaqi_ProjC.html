Instructions:

    Function 1: Users can switch between Phong Shading and Gouraud Shading, as well as between Phong lighting and Blinn-Phong lighting, 4 different combinations.

                                         

    Function 2: Users can select their favorite material for these four 3D objects from the drop-down bar, or drag the slider to adjust the "brightness (Shininess)" of the material.

    Ambient: R: G: B:
    Diffuse: R: G: B:
    Specular: R: G: B:

    Position:  X: Y: Z:

    Function 5: Use the 'W, A, S, D' on your keyboard to control the position of the camera and use the 'arrow keys' to adjust the aiming direction. Besides, users can also change the aiming directionby by dragging the mouse.
